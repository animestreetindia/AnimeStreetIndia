Privacy Policy

At Anime Street, the privacy of our visitors is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that is collected and recorded by Anime Street and how we use it.

Log Files

Anime Street follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected may include IP addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, and referring pages.

Cookies

Anime Street uses cookies to store information about visitors’ preferences and to optimize the user experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

Third Party Privacy Policies

Anime Street’s Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers.

Children’s Information

Anime Street does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child has provided this information on our website, please contact us and we will remove it immediately.
